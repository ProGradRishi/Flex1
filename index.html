HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

~ Made By ProGrad JR,Rishi Gautham


Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for
30 seconds will help you avoid germs.


Cover Your Mouth and Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or
coughing in public. Even better use a mask.


Stay Home and Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places.
Prefer staying indoors.

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food.
Eat as healthy as possible to boost your immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great
way to keep your self calm and composed.

BE AWARE,BE SAFE
